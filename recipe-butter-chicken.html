Classic Butter Chicken

A rich, creamy tomato-based curry that is perfect with naan or rice.

Prep Time
20 Mins
Cook Time
30 Mins
Servings
4 People

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 cup Tomato Puree
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Fresh Coriander (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    In a bowl, mix yogurt, 1 tbsp garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, and salt. Add chicken cubes and coat well. Let it sit for at least 15 minutes.
  2. Cook the Chicken:
    Heat a large pan over medium heat. Add a little oil and cook the chicken pieces until browned (about 5-7 minutes). Remove chicken from the pan and set aside.
  3. Make the Sauce:
    In the same pan, melt the butter. Pour in the tomato puree and the remaining 1 tbsp of garam masala. Simmer for 10 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  4. Combine:
    Add the cooked chicken back into the sauce. Stir well.
  5. Add Cream:
    Lower the heat and stir in the heavy cream. Simmer for another 2 minutes. Do not let it boil rapidly after adding cream.
  6. Serve:
    Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with Naan or Rice.

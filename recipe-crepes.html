Authentic French Cr√™pes

Paper-thin pancakes that are perfect for breakfast or dessert. Just add your favorite topping.

Prep Time
10 Mins
Cook Time
20 Mins
Servings
8 Cr√™pes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 2 tbsp Melted Butter
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • Butter (for pan coating)

Instructions

  1. Make the Batter:
    In a large mixing bowl, whisk the flour and the eggs. Gradually add in the milk and water while whisking to prevent lumps.
  2. Add Butter:
    Add the salt and the 2 tablespoons of melted butter. Beat until smooth.
  3. Rest (Important):
    Let the batter sit in the fridge for at least 30 minutes (or up to 1 hour). This relaxes the gluten and makes the cr√™pes tender.
  4. Heat the Pan:
    Heat a non-stick pan over medium-high heat. Lightly coat with butter.
  5. Pour and Swirl:
    Pour about 1/4 cup of batter into the center of the pan. Immediately lift the pan and swirl it in a circular motion so the batter coats the bottom evenly and thinly.
  6. Flip:
    Cook for about 1-2 minutes until the edges brown and lift up. Flip gently and cook the other side for 1 minute.
  7. Serve:
    Serve hot with lemon and sugar, Nutella, or jam.

About Us

Bringing the world's flavors to your kitchen table.

Our Mission

Discovery at your comfort.

We believe that you shouldn't have to travel thousands of miles or pay expensive restaurant bills to experience authentic global cuisine. Our mission is to simplify complex international recipes so you can explore the world without leaving your home.

Our Vision

Promoting health through home cooking.

Restaurant food often hides excess oil, sugar, and preservatives. We envision a world where people take control of their health by cooking fresh, nutritious meals, understanding exactly what goes into their bodies while still enjoying delicious flavors.

Why "At Home Outing"?

The idea started with a simple question: "Why do we wait for the weekend to eat something special?"

Cooking at home is often seen as a chore, while eating out is a treat. We want to change that mindset. By providing step-by-step guides for Indian, Japanese, and French cuisines, we turn your daily cooking routine into an exciting culinary adventure.

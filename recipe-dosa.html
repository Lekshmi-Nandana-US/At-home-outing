Crispy Masala Dosa

A South Indian staple. Thin, crispy fermented rice crepe stuffed with a savory spiced potato filling.

Prep Time
15 Mins
Cook Time
20 Mins
Servings
6 Dosas

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Dosa Batter (Store-bought or Homemade)
  • 4 large Potatoes (Boiled & Mashed)
  • 2 Onions (Sliced)
  • 2 Green Chilies
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • Curry Leaves
  • Oil or Ghee for roasting

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Filling (Aloo Masala):
    Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and curry leaves. Add onions and green chilies and saut√© until soft. Add turmeric, salt, and mashed potatoes. Mix well and set aside.
  2. Heat the Tawa (Griddle):
    Heat a non-stick or cast-iron tawa. It should be hot enough that water sizzles when sprinkled on it. Wipe it clean.
  3. Spread the Batter:
    Pour a ladle of batter in the center. Using the base of the ladle, spread the batter in a circular motion to make a thin crepe.
  4. Roast:
    Drizzle a teaspoon of oil or ghee around the edges. Cook until the base turns golden brown and crispy.
  5. Fold and Serve:
    Place a spoonful of the potato filling in the center. Fold the dosa over the filling. Serve hot with Coconut Chutney and Sambar.

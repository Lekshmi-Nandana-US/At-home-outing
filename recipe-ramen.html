Classic Shoyu Ramen

A light yet deep savory broth flavored with soy sauce, topped with tender pork and fresh noodles.

Prep Time
30 Mins
Cook Time
60 Mins
Servings
2 People

Ingredients

  • 2 packs Fresh Ramen Noodles
  • 4 cups Chicken Stock
  • 1 cup Dashi Stock
  • 3 tbsp Soy Sauce (Shoyu)
  • 1 tbsp Mirin
  • 1 tsp Ginger & Garlic (minced)
  • **Toppings:**
  • Soft Boiled Egg (Ajitsuke Tamago)
  • Green Onions (sliced)
  • Nori Sheets

Instructions

  1. Make the Soup Base:
    In a pot, combine chicken stock, dashi, ginger, garlic, soy sauce, and mirin. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes to let flavors meld. Taste and add salt if needed.
  2. Prepare Toppings:
    Boil eggs for exactly 6 minutes and 30 seconds for a soft yolk. Peel and slice. Chop green onions.
  3. Cook Noodles:
    Bring a large separate pot of water to a rolling boil. Add noodles. Fresh noodles only need about 1-2 minutes. Drain well.
  4. Assemble:
    Place noodles in a serving bowl. Pour the hot broth over the noodles.
  5. Garnish:
    Top with the soft boiled egg, green onions, and a sheet of nori on the side. Serve immediately while piping hot.

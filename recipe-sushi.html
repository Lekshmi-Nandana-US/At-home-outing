Maki Sushi Rolls

Fun to make and delicious to eat. Customize your filling with cucumber, avocado, or salmon.

Prep Time
40 Mins
Cook Time
20 Mins
Servings
3 Rolls

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Sushi Rice (Short Grain)
  • 1/3 cup Rice Vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Sugar + 1 tsp Salt
  • 3 sheets Nori (Seaweed)
  • **Fillings:**
  • Cucumber (cut into strips)
  • Avocado (sliced)
  • Fresh Salmon or Crab stick
  • Soy Sauce & Wasabi for dipping

Instructions

  1. Prepare Rice:
    Wash rice until water runs clear. Cook rice in a cooker or pot. Once cooked, mix the vinegar, sugar, and salt, and fold it gently into the hot rice. Let it cool to room temperature.
  2. Prepare Mat:
    Place a bamboo rolling mat on the table. Place a sheet of Nori on top, shiny side down.
  3. Layer Rice:
    Wet your hands to stop sticking. Spread a thin layer of rice over the Nori, leaving a 1-inch gap at the top edge.
  4. Add Fillings:
    Place your cucumber, avocado, and fish in a line near the bottom edge of the rice.
  5. Roll:
    Lift the bamboo mat from the bottom and roll it over the filling, tucking it in tightly. Continue rolling until you reach the empty Nori strip at the top. Wet the strip with water to seal the roll.
  6. Cut:
    Use a very sharp, wet knife to slice the roll into bite-sized pieces.

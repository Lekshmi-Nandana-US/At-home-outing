Rustic Ratatouille

A healthy, comforting Proven√ßal vegetable stew bursting with summer flavors.

Prep Time
20 Mins
Cook Time
40 Mins
Servings
4 People

Ingredients

  • 1 Eggplant (cubed)
  • 1 Zucchini (cubed)
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper (chopped)
  • 1 Yellow Onion (chopped)
  • 3 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 1 can Crushed Tomatoes (400g)
  • 1 tbsp Herbes de Provence
  • Olive Oil
  • Fresh Basil (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prep Vegetables:
    Wash and cut all vegetables into roughly same-sized chunks (about 1 inch). This ensures they cook evenly.
  2. Saut√© Onions:
    Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the onions and cook until soft (5 mins). Add the garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  3. Cook Veggies:
    Add the peppers and eggplant. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until the eggplant starts to soften. Add the zucchini last (it cooks fastest).
  4. Simmer:
    Pour in the crushed tomatoes and sprinkle the Herbes de Provence, salt, and pepper. Stir well. Cover the pot and reduce heat to low. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Finish:
    Remove the lid and cook for another 5-10 minutes to let the sauce thicken. Taste and adjust salt.
  6. Serve:
    Serve warm or at room temperature (traditional), garnished with fresh basil. Great with crusty bread.
